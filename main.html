About ArogyaVani

Arogyavani is a telemedicine platform designed to bridge healthcare accessibility gaps for illiterate and semi-literate individuals in rural India, utilizing voice commands, image recognition, and community support through Asha workers and local facilities like post offices, Gram Panchayats, and schools.
